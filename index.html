We’re set on making our wines true to the place they grow. The winemaking is simple - good estate grown fruit, organic & biodynamic, handpicked, vineyard derived yeasts, no additives or fining, minimal sulphur at bottling and where possible, no filtration. Then we have character.

Black Estate is a remarkable place for wine.

The season's fruit has been picked, fermented and is now wintering over in barrel or tank. We had perfect weather for picking and harvest was calm and, well quite lovely with a smaller than usual yield due to poor flowering. The quality of the havest is exciting. Now we and our wine rest...until pruning in June and the wines stirring in spring.
